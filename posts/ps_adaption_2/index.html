Exploring Polyphasic Sleep: My Adaptation Day 2

Posted on Jun 18, 2023

Here’s a concise account of my first day of sleep experimentation:

Despite attempts, I was unable to take a nap on Day 1. Falling asleep during the day proved more difficult than expected. However, when it came to the core sleep period, I had no issues drifting off.

Interestingly, I awoke at 3:21 AM, a nearly 10 minutes before my alarm was due to ring. It’s fascinating how attuned our bodies can be to internal rhythms.

Upon waking, I felt moderately fresh. I wouldn’t describe myself as fully revitalized, but I wasn’t dragging either - somewhere in the middle.

As I step into Day 2, I’m hoping to successfully include a nap in my routine. Stay tuned to see if the elusive daytime sleep becomes a reality!
