Exploring Polyphasic Sleep: My Adaptation Day 4

Posted on Jun 20, 2023

On Day 3, during my first scheduled nap, I found it difficult to fall asleep. Despite feeling tired, my mind seemed to resist the embrace of sleep. However, things took a positive turn during my midday nap. I was able to enjoy a good nap.

Unfortunately, as the day progressed, I again experienced mild headaches during midday and early afternoon. These headaches, combined with a constant feeling of tiredness and weakness, made it challenging to stay focused and productive. Nonetheless, as the evening approached, the headaches started to subside, bringing some relief.

When it was time for my core sleep, I faced difficulty falling asleep as quickly as I had the previous day. This delayed onset of sleep prolonged the time it took for me to enter a deep, restorative sleep state.

Yesterday, I started questioning whether this adaptation is worth it. But for now, I will continue and see what the next few days will be like. Eventually, I will extend the core sleep time by one cycle to 6 hours and only nap once a day.

This morning, getting up was a bit harder; I unintentionally overslept by half an hour, waking up at 4 a.m. I still felt a bit fatigued upon awakening.
